= properties of a meme that make it spread more and live longer
Memetic properties can be divided into a few broad classes:
Memorable = easy to remember/understand
Spreadable = people want to spread the meme
Durable = hard to get rid of
Aggressive = fights competing memes
# **Memorable+Spreadable:
**
Properties that increase memorability and spreadability:
Emotional
Negative emotion is stronger than positive emotion here
Excitingness is an exception — very powerful positive emotion.
Shame increases memorability but decreases spreadability
For ideologies in particular, guilt can be very powerful.
Simple
Tribal
Humans are wired to think in terms of ingroup vs outgroup.
Practically Valuable
Narrative-like
Stories/narratives are more memorable abstract ideas
Memes can be stories, or *encoded within stories
*Triggerable
=experiences/thoughts bring that meme to mind
(For humans in particular): deals with sex,food, or power 
# **Spreadable
**
Status-raising
AKA ‘social currency’
Public
Pro-self-spreading
= contains the message “you should spread this meme”
Ambitious
= makes people more ambitious, or selects for ambitious people.
# **Durable
**
Uncounterable
= no appealing countermemes
Exemple: “Breast Cancer Awareness”
Adaptable
= meme can change gradually (”mutate”) as the world changes, *without losing its identity
*Example: Conservatism is adaptable, Liberalism isn’t
Sexual
= meme can change by integrating with other memes
Example: Americanism is sexual, Nazism isn’t.
Addictive
"You are a member of marginalized identity group Y; anyone who notices facts that could be construed to call this narrative into question is thereby hurting you by __invalidating your identity__."
# **Aggressive
**
Censoring
= promotes censoring opposing memes
“anti free speech”
