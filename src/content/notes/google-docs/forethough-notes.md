Perhaps my two biggest passions are philosophy and AI. I have a good technical understanding of how LLMs and AI systems work. This technical understanding is important, but I think that getting stuck in the technical view of these things is a trap. “Alignment is a technical problem” is a common refrain, but it’s not entirely true; alignment is both a technical and philosophical problem. In short, I think that a holistic view of AI is required in order to understand it and predict what ASI outcomes are going to look like. I think I’ve figured out the right questions to ask (and if I haven’t, I want to know!), and it turns out they’re philosophical questions.
