We live in a period of very high X-risk per time. Highly capable AI that isn’t quite ASI level poses a huge risk: superviruses, wide scale poisoning, etc. 
Also, an underrated source of X-risk is religion/cult takeover that stablizes into an equilibrium. This is far more likely to happen if the religion/cult is anti technology, as technology is one of the big forces in social change – one of the big equilibrium disrupters. Alternatively, the religion/cult might pivot to ludditeism after world domination.
