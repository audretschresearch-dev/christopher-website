For most people, here's how it works.

If you're a type, then others can discern your social value by comparing you to other people of your type.

If you are typeless, people don't have anything to compare you with. They cannot figure out "who you are"; they don't know how to evaluate you.

Types can be interpreted in two ways:

1. Types are attractors in personality space.
2. Types are social roles / characters. A type tells you who you should be and what you should do.
